Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 2026

Kestrel AI ("we", "us", or "our") operates the satellite object detection platform available at this website. This policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information.

Information We Collect

When you create an account or use our service, we collect:

  • Account information: name, company, job title, phone number, and email address
  • Search data: addresses, coordinates, and detection results associated with your searches
  • Usage data: search counts, timestamps, and plan information
  • Payment information: processed securely by Stripe — we do not store card details

How We Use Your Information

  • To provide and operate the detection service
  • To enforce usage limits and manage your subscription
  • To send transactional emails (account confirmation, billing receipts)
  • To improve detection accuracy and platform performance

Data Storage

Your data is stored in Supabase (SOC 2 Type II compliant), encrypted in transit (TLS) and at rest (AES-256). Satellite imagery used in searches is stored in private buckets accessible only to your account.

Third-Party Services

  • Google Maps Static API — used to fetch satellite imagery for address-based searches
  • Stripe — payment processing
  • Supabase — database and authentication
  • Hugging Face — ML inference infrastructure

Data Retention

Search history is retained according to your plan (90 days for Starter, 1 year for Professional, unlimited for Enterprise). Account data is retained for as long as your account is active. You may request deletion at any time.

Your Rights

You may request access to, correction of, or deletion of your personal data by contacting us at contact@satellitevision.ai.

Contact

Questions about this policy? Email us at contact@satellitevision.ai.
