02 · What it does

Built for shops that show up.

Quotebird isn't a spreadsheet bolted onto a payment processor. It's three tools that finally know about each other — a catalog, a quoting AI, and an invoicing layer — wrapped in something that opens fast on a five-year-old phone.

01

Your catalog,
ingested.

Snap a photo or upload a PDF of your supplier price sheet. Claude Vision extracts every SKU code, description, unit, and price. You review the rows, approve them, and the catalog is yours forever.

05 · The screen you'll live in

Type the job. Done.

Customer fields up top — optional but encouraged. A big honest textarea for the job description. Tap a chip for an example. Generate.

A

Optional customer info

Name, email, phone, address — fill what you have. The quote can be drafted without them and emailed later.

B

The job, in your words

Plain English. The AI handles the structure, the line-item math, and the markup. You handle the trade knowledge.
